~ The Founder ~

Emily Albert

Favorite Since 2012

The inspiration for all our desserts at La Fiesta began with my Spanish mom. She had a way of surprising our family with homemade cakes and sweets all the time and having parties all the time. It was her thoughtfulness and my sweet tooth that inspired me to start baking at an early age and want to lunch my businsess that helps people to enjoy parties and great desserts with each other.

In 20010, shortly after I graduated culinary school, and met my wonderful husband who encouraged me and supported me to open La Fiesta, I lunched my business and opened my doors for all the people who want to enjoy and have fun with each other and create unforgatable moments with their loved ones.

~ About Us ~

Our owner, started La Fiest Cake Shope in 2012 with the simple belief that baked goods make people smile. From birthday cakes to wedding desserts, La Fiesta’s team of expert bakers and decorators strive to create unforgettable memories with every cake they bake. With a focus on quality and customer service, Emily and her team have created a bakery that is the go-to for delicious treats in the Colorado Springs area. Whether it’s a birthday cake, wedding dessert, or any other celebration, La Fiesta Cake Shope team will make sure your event is unforgettable. Stop by today and taste the difference!
