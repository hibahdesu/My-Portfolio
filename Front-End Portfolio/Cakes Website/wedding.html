Wedding


We believe that the wedding cake or dessert bar is the centerpiece of the reception, and it should be just as unique and wonderful as you are! Whether your inspiration comes from a sweet memory or a Pinterest board, we work with you closely to ensure that we capture the essence of your vision.

When you sit down with one of the members of our knowledgeable staff for a complimentary tasting, you will have the opportunity to taste 8 of our delicious fillings as well as 4 of our delectable flavors of cake. When it comes to your wedding day, a trained staff member can deliver, and set up your cake, so it looks exactly as you’ve always pictured it.

We look at each of our cakes as an edible piece of art, where we can express a love story. We would love to tell yours!


~ Wedding Pricing and Options ~

Our weddings cakes start at $5.50 per serving for buttercream icing and $6.50 per serving for fondant, and go up from there depending on the complexity of design chosen. We also have a dessert bar menu full of delicious dessert options (including cupcakes) HERE .

If it would be helpful, please visit our wedding cake or dessert bar portfolio pages to see examples of our work and gather inspiration! Our master decorators can also replicate any design you have seen on Pinterest, Google, or in a magazine.

We recommend booking your wedding at least 3-6 months in advance to ensure you are are able to reserve your specific date, but we are always happy to accommodate later bookings as well!

Please Note: A 30% deposit is required to reserve your order date. This deposit is non-refundable.


~ Wedding Tastings ~

The best way to get an idea of how much your cake or dessert bar will cost is to set up a cake tasting and consultation. Try delicious cake and/or dessert options while meeting with our designer to get a custom design and quote. Call us or email us by clicking here or schedule your appointment online by going to: Book Appointment

**Please Note: Tastings/Consultations are complimentary for orders of 25 servings or more. There is a $15 per plate tasting fee for cutting cake tastings, or orders less than 25 servings.**

If you have questions or would you like a pre-quote for the purposes of budgeting, please use our contact form below or email us directly at Weddings@SugarplumCakeShoppe.com. We will contact you within 24 – 48 hours. Should you decide that The Sugarplum Cake Shoppe is your vendor of choice, we can provide a more specific quote based on you personal cake consultation.

Thank you for this opportunity. We are so glad you found us and are excited to talk with you!
